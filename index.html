Why This Exists

The Challenge You Know

Student government leadership turns over every year. Institutional knowledge walks out the door. Each new president starts from scratch, solving problems their predecessors already solved. NMSGA is the solution we're building together — continuity across schools and across time.

I

Peer-to-Peer Connection

Direct access to student body presidents at other medical schools. Ask questions, share experiences, and learn from peers who understand exactly what the role demands.

II

Institutional Knowledge

A shared library of templates, bylaws, event guides, and governance tools — contributed by members and preserved for future leaders. What works at one school can work at yours.

III

A Unified Voice

When student governments coordinate nationally, we become stronger partners in shaping medical education. NMSGA gives us the infrastructure to collaborate constructively at scale.

Member Benefits

What You Get

Practical resources and real connections that help you lead more effectively.

Resource Library

Templates, bylaws, event guides, and governance tools contributed by members. Build on what's already working at other institutions.

Group Purchasing

Collective buying power across member schools means better pricing on services and vendors that student governments commonly use.

Private Community

A dedicated space to connect with other student body presidents — ask questions, share solutions, and get advice from peers who understand the role.

Best Practices

Regular updates on what's working at member schools — successful initiatives, lessons learned, and ideas worth adapting to your institution.
